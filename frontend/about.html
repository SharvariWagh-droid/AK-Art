Meet The Artist-Abhilasha Khatri

Abhilasha is a children’s book illustrator, based in India. She has illustrated picture books and middle grade novels for several international authors and publishers. She loves to experiment with drawing styles and colour palettes with every project she works on.

She holds a Masters degree in Architecture from Texas Tech University, USA and after working as an architect for a decade, she found her true calling in the world of children’s books. Abhilasha is a passionate and spirited artist who loves to talk through her art.

​
